#### **GLOW's Cal-Mag** (**5** *mL/L*)

  - Measure out 750 ml RO water
  - Add 4 grams Calcium Chloride and stir until fully dissolved.  
  - Add 6 grams Magnesium Nitrate and stir until fully dissolved.  
  - Add 23 grams Calcium Nitrate and stir until fully dissolved.  
  - Top up to 1 liter.

- This is a high concentrate formulation based upon analysis concentrations. The EC of the Part A is 1.08, Part B EC = 1.64, combined the  
EC is 2.72. Obviously a higher EC then most would consider using.
- The pH used with RO water should be 5.4 at 1:100
- The TDS at a 1:100 mix is 1904
- This recipe is for a x100 concentrate. That means a combination of 9.46353 Liters of Part A. And 9.46353 Liters of Part B, to which the trace  
nutrients are added. With all mixed together in a dry mix you have the dry mix equivalent of Floro Nova Bloom without the added humus.
